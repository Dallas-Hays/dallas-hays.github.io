About Me

Education

I graduated from CU Boulder in May 2015, with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with emphasis in Networked Devices and Systems (NDS). The NDS track teaches the basics of being a network professional in particular how to design and deploy a network infrastructure. Through the courses I gained extensive knowledge of networking practices and was able to obtain my CCNA certification (Certified Cisco Network Associate) in January 2015.

In addition to networking, this track has a focus on security. I am passionate about all aspects of security and hope to expand my focus in this area in the future. As a result I have participated in a recent Hackathon at CU in Spring 2015 where we exploited linux based machines through various vulnerabilities ranging from kernel exploits to web command injections and some kernel exploits as well.
